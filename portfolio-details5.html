Attending + Exchange with ULIS

In my high school freshman year, my family made a significant move from New York to Portugal, prompted by both the COVID-19 pandemic and the opportunity to care for my overseas grandparents. Though what initially inspired the idea, this transition wasn't just about their health; it was a chance for our family to embark on an entirely new adventure together. I spent the latter half of my freshman year at United Lisbon International School (ULIS), and those six months served as a revelation, transforming my perspective in numerous ways. Words can hardly do justice to the amazing people I met and the precious memories I formed during my time there. Subsequently, our connection with the school led to the creation of an exchange program with my "home" school, Mineola High School, allowing my two worlds to collide in the best possible way. If I had to pinpoint a single period in my life that played a pivotal role in shaping who I am today, it would undoubtedly be my time spent at ULIS.
